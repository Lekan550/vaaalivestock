Our Services

Comprehensive livestock and agribusiness solutions

Livestock Production & Breeding

We specialize in the breeding, rearing, and production of high-quality livestock including poultry, small ruminants (goats and sheep), and large ruminants (cattle). Our modern farms are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to ensure optimal animal health and productivity.

  • Modern farm facilities with climate control
  • Quality breeding stock selection
  • Improved feeding and nutrition programs
  • Regular health monitoring and vaccination
  • Record keeping and performance tracking
Learn More

Processing & Packaging

Our processing facility handles the transformation of raw livestock products into market-ready items. We maintain strict hygiene standards and quality control throughout the processing chain.

  • Meat processing and packaging
  • Milk collection and pasteurization
  • Cheese and dairy product production
  • Hide and skin processing
  • Hygienic packaging for local and export markets
Learn More

Consultancy & Training Services

We provide expert consultancy and training services to farmers, investors, and stakeholders in the livestock industry. Our programs are designed to improve productivity and profitability.

  • Farm setup and management consultancy
  • Animal health and disease management training
  • Feed formulation and nutrition workshops
  • Agribusiness development programs
  • On-site technical support and extension services

Sales & Distribution

We offer comprehensive sales and distribution services for livestock, feed ingredients, veterinary supplies, and farm equipment across Nigeria.
